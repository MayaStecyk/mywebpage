Fun Facts

A panda can poop up to 28 kilograms a day, 7 times the daily average of human poop

A pandas whole day consists of only sleeping and eating

Pandas can climb and swim

Many years ago, pandas were actually carnivores

1 panda year is the equivalent to 3 human years

Baby pandas are born pink

A newborn panda is approximately the same size as a stick of butter

Unlike koalas, pandas are real bears

Pandas actually don't hibernate, but they do get their 10 hours of sleep a night
