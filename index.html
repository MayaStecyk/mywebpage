Pandas

Pandas are a species originating from China resembling a bear, as it is often called the Panda Bear, however, it is not actually a bear.
They are recognized for their black markings over their white fur, specifically the oval ones around their eyes.
Pandas have said to be originally carnivorous, but now, they have sustained a better diet by going herbivorous and only eating bamboo of the bamboo forests of China.
Pandas have famously become endangered, and that is mostly beacuse of poachers, but also because of how hard it is for Pandas to reproduce.
